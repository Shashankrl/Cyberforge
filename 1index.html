How to Protect Yourself During an Earthquake

Earthquakes can be unpredictable and dangerous, but staying prepared can save lives. Here are some quick tips:

  • Drop to the ground, cover your head and neck with your arms, and hold on to sturdy furniture.
  • Stay away from windows, mirrors, and anything that could shatter or fall.
  • If you're outside, move to an open area away from buildings and power lines.
  • If you're driving, pull over and stop in a safe location, avoiding bridges and overpasses.
